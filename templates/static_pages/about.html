About PrepEnhancer

PrepEnhancer is an AI-powered educational platform designed to revolutionize the way you learn. Our mission is to provide students and lifelong learners with cutting-edge tools that make preparation faster, smarter, and more interactive.

Our Vision

We believe that static content like PDFs and long videos shouldn't be a barrier to understanding. By leveraging state-of-the-art LLMs and Sarvam AI, we transform traditional study materials into dynamic, personalized learning experiences.

What We Offer

  • AI MCQ Generator: Instant high-quality questions from any PDF.
  • Video Summarizer: Quick insights and key takeaways from YouTube videos.
  • Audio Content Generator: Professional podcasts and storytelling from your topics.
  • Mock Tests: Comprehensive testing environment to track your progress.

Whether you're preparing for competitive exams or exploring a new hobby, PrepEnhancer is your ultimate learning companion.
