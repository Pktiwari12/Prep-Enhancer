Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the MCQ Generator work?

Simply upload a PDF document. Our AI analyzes the text and generates relevant multiple-choice questions with detailed explanations based on the content.

2. Is there a limit to the video length for summarization?

Our tool works best with videos that have English or Hindi transcripts. While there's no strict limit, extremely long videos might take longer to process.

Yes! Every audio file generated using our AI Audio tool can be played directly on the site or downloaded for offline listening.

We prioritize your privacy. Uploaded PDFs and generated content are stored securely and associated only with your user account.
